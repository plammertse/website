Matlab scripts

Over the years, I have collected a number of Matlab scripts. The tables below list some of them.

These scripts are sometimes referenced from other pages on this website. Others are just along for the ride.

As usual, they come here warts and all, and with zero guarantee. If they contain functions or objects (classes), they may have test or example code in a commented section at the end of the file.

Feel free to browse and copy any selection of them if you find them useful. If you don't have Matlab, you might want to run them in Octave Online, or ask ChatGPT to convert them to Python for you.

Depending on your system settings, you may open an editor on them by left clicking the link, or save them to your disk by right clicking and selecting "Save link as..".
