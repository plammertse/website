Matlab scripts

Over the years, I have collected a number of Matlab scripts. The subdirectory below contains some of them.

These scripts are sometimes referenced from other pages on this website. Some are just along for the ride.

Feel free to browse and copy any selection of them if you find them useful. If you don't have Matlab, you might want to run them in Octave Online, or ask ChatGPT to convert them to Python for you.

As usual, they come here warts and all, and with zero guarantee.

If they contain functions or objects (classes), they may have test code at the end.
