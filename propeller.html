Propellers

Ever since trying to fly rubber powered models as a kid, I have had a keen interest in propellers.

In university, I followed the mesmerizing lectures by prof. Dobbinga on the subject. Later I found articles by Eugene Larrabee on the enormous increase in performance of a paddle-shaped propeller over straight blades in the "Gossamer Condor" man-powered aircraft. It made the difference between a flight of 1 mile maximum and a flight from Crete to Santorini.
