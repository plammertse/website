The Center of Percussion

Classical Mechanics has a number of useful concepts. A very basic one is the Center of Gravity (CG), more properly called Center of Mass (COM).

A lesser known and somewhat less basic concept is the Center of Percussion (CP).

The usual examples are the hand reaction of a baseball bat or tennis racket, or a hammer on anvil.
Interestingly, the concept is also useful in visualizing the symmetrical short period mode of airplanes.
