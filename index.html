Purpose of this site

This site gives musings on things that have caught my attention over the years.

The fun is often in applying basic principles to clarify otherwise well known facts.

Other parts are unashamedly autobiographical.

Feel free to use any of this material, but do so at your own risk...

Some of it is tested in practice, but none of it is peer reviewed.
