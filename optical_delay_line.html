TBW. Daily (nightly) travel of 50 m or so.
Tight control versus lazy control.
Mechanical low pass filter. Connection to acoustics.
Avoid mechanical damping because it transmits high frequencies. Connection to mass-only model in admittance haptics, Parthiban on its stability, and to hand control of spiral mode and phugoid.
