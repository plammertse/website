Intro

In the 1980's, Etkin's book was the classic on Flight Dynamics, or as it was also called "Aircraft Stability and Control". The edition of choice was the 2n edition, but see the tab on Etkin editions.

Etkin was a professor at UTIAS in Toronto, which I believe was a center of excellence at the time, especially in flight simulation. There was a line of descendancy in Toronto from Etkin, to Reid, to Nahon and Kirdeikis and later Advani, in motion cueing algorithms for flight simulation, which I think inspired the work in Delft on high quality motion systems with hydrostatic bearings.

Try out the tabs above for background information on the books, especially the 3d edition, and how best to use them.
