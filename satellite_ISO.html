The ISO satellite

From **** to **** I was involved in the "Attitude and Orbit Control System" (AOCS) of the ISO satellite.

ISO was an ESA scientific (astronomical) satellite : "Infrared Space Observatory". Fokker (FSS) did the attitude control system as a subcontractor for AéroSpatiale.

My job was to run the control simulations. I implemented the spec for the onboard ADA software in FORTRAN, and ran it with dynamical models of the satellite's orbit, sensors, and actuators.

This was interesting enough in its own right, bit at the end of the project I contributed an actual improvement in the attitude (pointing) control law.



P. Lammertse, 2025
